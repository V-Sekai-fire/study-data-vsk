- finishability: repo has 1 open issue (+6), 2 (+4), 3 (+2), ≤5 (+1); closing empties the repo.

- doability: ≤1 comment (+2)/≤3 (+1); short body (+1); finishing-task title (+2); good-first/help-wanted/docs label (+2); epic title (−4); epic/needs-design label (−3).
- elixir: Elixir repo (+4); Elixir/BEAM keywords in title (+3); CLI-shaped task (+2), since any CLI ships as an Elixir binary via Burrito. Ties break toward Elixir.
- Excluded: archived or fork repos, and issues labelled blocked/wontfix/invalid/duplicate.
